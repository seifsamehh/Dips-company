WE ARE DIPS

Delta Inspection & Petroleum services (DIPS), is a shareholder company has been founded & established year 2014. One of the leading companies in the MENA region in providing the Rope Access Service, moreover we are recognized by nearly 6 accreditation bodies. So, our strong portfolio enabled us to serve most of the biggest companies worldwide, inside Egypt & overseas as well (offshore and onshore). Delta Inspection & Petroleum services (DIPS), is serving in the oil & Gas field, Petrochemicals & power industries projects. We have the pleasure to serve big projects, in fixed basis, in several countries, For instance, Saudi Arabia, Kuwait, Italy, Ghana, Kazakhstan, Libya, Cot devoir, …etc. by using our well qualified man power. Nowadays, we can announce that Delta Inspection & Petroleum services (DIPS) became one of the top among all companies working in the inspection services, in the MENA region, through its Agent offices.

We are applying IMS – Integrated Management System – in our line of business

  • We are ISO Certified Accredited by LIYODS Register: ISO 9001: 2015
  • We are “ISO 45001: 2018” Accredited by LIYODS register.
  • We are “ISO 14001: 2015” certified, Environmental Management System – Accredited by LIYODS register.
  • We are certified of full Membership, Lifting Equipment Engineers Association – LEEA – Certificate No. 6455.
  • We are an IRATA International Membership, for Rope Access (Operator & Trainer) – Certificate No. 7108/OT.
  • We are formally recognized as a member of Drops, the industry wide initiative focused on preventing dropped objects in the workplace.
  • DIPS is licensed from the Egyptian Nuclear & Radiological Regulatory Authority (ENRRA) by 10 Gamma Ray sources.
  • We are applying for ABS Certificate, for Hull Gauging for vessels.

According to the A/M accreditation certification we are granted. We became capable in the following:

  • DIPS is certified by LEEA for inspecting and certifying various lifting equipment and accessories. With our NDT Level II certified inspectors, which are performing inspections according to BS, ASTM, ASME, API and LOLER.
  • DIPS is certified by IRATA association with our Level III, Level II and Level I certified inspectors in rope access work at height.
  • DIPS has several inspection facilities in Egypt that will satisfy our customer needs.
  • DIPS has the flexibility to transfer its calibrated equipment and certified Inspectors overseas according to the customer requirements.
  • DIPS has its training avenue approved by IRATA International in Suez Free zone, as its venue can train a large number of applicants per training course.
  • DIPS has a very professional team; our aim is to satisfy and meet our customer needs in 24/7 in a very quick responsive way.
